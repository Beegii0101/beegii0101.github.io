About Me

Hello! I'm Binderiya (Beegii), a passionate Japanese language teacher from Mongolia. I passed JLPT N1 in just 1 year and 9 months, and now I help Mongolian students achieve their Japanese learning goals.

With experience studying in 4 countries (Mongolia, Japan, USA, Italy), I bring a unique multicultural perspective to language teaching. I graduated from J.F. Oberlin University in Tokyo with a degree in Global Communication.

Beyond teaching, I'm a musician (Morin khuur), content creator, and founder of the Tokitenku Foundation, which supports Mongolians in Japan. I share my journey and lessons on my YouTube channel "Beegii Chuuki."
