About Tokitenku Yoshiaki

Tokitenku Yoshiaki (時天空 慶晃), born as Ariunbuyan Khuchitbaatar, was a professional sumo wrestler from Mongolia who competed in the top division of sumo wrestling.

Throughout his career, Tokitenku demonstrated exceptional dedication, discipline, and cultural bridge-building between Mongolia and Japan. His legacy extends beyond the sumo ring through his commitment to supporting young Mongolians.

The Tokitenku Foundation was established to honor his memory and continue his mission of empowering Mongolian youth through education, cultural exchange, and community support.

His values of perseverance, respect, and giving back to the community continue to inspire our work today.
